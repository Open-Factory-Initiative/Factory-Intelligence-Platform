# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in the Open Factory Initiative a respectful, harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful and constructive
- Welcoming new contributors
- Giving clear feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy toward others
- Respecting different levels of manufacturing, software, and AI experience

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment or discrimination
- Personal attacks
- Trolling or insulting comments
- Publishing private information without permission
- Sharing confidential plant/customer data
- Dismissing safety concerns without discussion

## Manufacturing Data and Safety

This community may discuss industrial systems and manufacturing workflows. Contributors must avoid sharing proprietary, confidential, unsafe, or sensitive operational details from real facilities.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior.

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, and pull requests that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct.
